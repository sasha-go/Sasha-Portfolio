I’m a full stack developer and recent graduate from Thinkful’s Nights and Weekends Engineering program. I began my career providing world class customer support at GoFundMe and I soon witnessed how meaningful a product could positively impact users in the real world. Overtime, I became passionate about improving the quality of our product and learning more about its inner workings. Luckily, I was surrounded by teammates who inspired me to reach beyond my limits and encouraged me to contribute on a level I never thought was possible.

This quickly led to my transition into Quality Engineering and now Full Stack Development. I'm dedicated to building software products that cater to the user experience and leave a positive imprint on our world.

When I’m not coding, you can find me at the dog park with my rescue pup, at the climbing gym with friends, or cozily curled up reading a book. I’m a life long learner who thrives when presented with a big challenge, whether that be a marathon or coding bootcamp.
